Two years ago, a small but ambitious team set out to prove that world-class AI technology doesn’t have to come from billion-dollar labs — it can come from people who understand what real businesses need.

Built by an experienced AI engineer and a sales professional from top-tier companies, our journey began with a simple goal: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and the daily operations of modern sales teams. We saw how much potential was being lost in manual outreach, cold calls, and disconnected CRM systems — and decided to change that.

Today, our company stands as a growing mid-size business that blends advanced technology with human-centric thinking. Over the past two years, we’ve built and refined AI systems that don’t just automate — they understand, learn, and sell. Our flagship platform, Dealvox AI, was born from that vision: an AI-powered sales voice assistant that runs on top leading large language models and connects seamlessly with the world’s most powerful AI service backends.

Dealvox AI represents the culmination of everything we believe in — practical intelligence, scalable innovation, and the relentless pursuit of simpler, smarter workflows. What started as a small experiment in AI-driven sales transformation has grown into a trusted solution helping businesses close more deals, faster, and with less friction.

We’re not here to impress with buzzwords. We’re here to make AI work for people — efficiently, intelligently, and at scale.
